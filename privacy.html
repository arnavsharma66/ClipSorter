Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

What is ClipSorter?

ClipSorter is a personal video clip organizer for social media creators. It uses Google OAuth to access your Google Drive and Google Sheets, and uses the Gemini API to analyze and tag video clips.

What data do we collect?

ClipSorter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays within your own Google account.

Google API usage

ClipSorter requests access to the following Google APIs:

  • Google Drive — to upload and store your video clips in your own Drive
  • Google Sheets — to maintain a searchable index of your clips in your own Sheet

ClipSorter's use of Google APIs is limited to these purposes only. Your data is never shared with third parties.

Gemini API

ClipSorter uses your own Gemini API key (provided by you) to analyze video clips. Video data is sent directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API. ClipSorter does not proxy or store this data.

Local storage

ClipSorter stores your Gemini API key in your browser's localStorage for convenience. This data never leaves your device.
