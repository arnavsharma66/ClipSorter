The Product

Your Personal AI Video Organizer

ClipSorter is an AI-powered personal video clip organizer built for speed. You drop your short B-roll and video clips into the app. Gemini AI instantly analyzes the footage and tags them with precise mood, action, subject, setting, and style.

They get securely stored in your personal Google Drive. When you need the perfect clip for a post, you simply paste your narrative or tweet text—ClipSorter understands the vibe and retrieves the exact visual match.
